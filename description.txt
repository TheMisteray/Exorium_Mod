Exorium Mod is an in-development mod somewhat based upon a Dungeons & Dragons campaign I ran. This mod is in its early stages and currently only contains pre-hardmode content.

This mod currently adds
- 1 Biome
- 2 Structures
- 1 Miniboss
- 3 Bosses
- 30+ Weapons
- 1 Town NPC
- And more
